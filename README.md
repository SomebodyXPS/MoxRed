# MoxRed: Your AI Server Engineer

Welcome to MoxRed, the ultimate AI-powered server engineer for your Minecraft server! MoxRed seamlessly connects your Discord community with your Minecraft server, allowing you to build, configure, automate, maintain, and improve server functionality through conversation.

## Why MoxRed?
- **Build with Natural Language:** Describe what you want, and MoxRed will build it. From ticket systems to daily rewards, create complex server workflows through conversation instead of manual coding or configuration editing.
- **Deep Integrations:** Connect Minecraft, Discord, webhooks, databases, and plugin events together seamlessly. MoxRed learns your server's plugins, commands, permissions, and APIs to build compatible automations.
- **Smart Follow-ups & Validation:** MoxRed asks clarifying questions when requirements are unclear before implementing anything, enforcing permissions, validation, and safety before applying changes.
- **Flexible AI Power:** Choose your favorite AI engine to power MoxRed, including OpenAI, Google Gemini, Anthropic Claude, and more.
